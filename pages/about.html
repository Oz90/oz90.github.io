About Me

My name is Özgür Baserdem and I'm currently an aspiring full stack web developer. Before I began the journey of learning how to code I studied political science at Stockholms Unversity and worked within the public sector in Sweden which has been an amazing experience where I met lots of amazing people and learned a lot. However, during my paternal leave I had the time to reflect and think about where I really wanted to make a career. Basically I had two options: stay within my current field and make a career where I am "safe" and not really evolve professionally in a way I want or change career into something I find exciting, fun and challenging. I've chosen the latter because I believe that it will make me happier to do something I find meaningful and where I feel like I'm evolving professionally in a direction I want. Therefore I've taken my first serious steps into the world of web development by applying to Nackademin's Full Stack Web Development programme which spans over 2 years where I'll learn programming both in theory and in practice. But probably most important of all I'll get the chance to learn programming at a real workplace during my internship, which I am looking forward too!

Where I am now

Nackademin

So currently, I am still taking my first steps of becoming a Full Stack Web Developer and I'm doing it at Nackademin. Nackademin is a higher vocational education school in Stockholm, Sweden. Nackademin was founded back in 1994 by two teachers who had an idea of starting an education that had a close collaboration and relation to the companies and industries it was educating students for. They thought that this would benefit the companies as well as the students and I personally believe in this strongly and it is also the reason why I chose to study at Nackademin because I think that Nackademins long experience and their ties to the IT-industry is very valuable when it's time for me to do my internship.

Where I am headed

Full Stack Web Development

At Nackademin I am currently in the Full Stack Open Source Web Developer programme which I chose because I wanted to learn both the front-end and back-end parts of web development. I believe that this gives me an edge in my career as I'll be able to understand large parts of the development process and hopefully be able to adjust accordingly depending on what is needed in the teams I work. I'll things like NodeJS, PHP, JavaScript, React, HMTL, CSS and SQL. This basically means that I'll have knowledge in how to program a browser (JavaScript), program a server (NodeJS) and program a database (SQL). I hope that my broad range of qualities will give me a great understanding of the different parts of web development as well as give me the possibility to help other team members, as well as understand them better. I am very much looking forward to putting my skills to work during my first internship starting in the fall of 2021.
